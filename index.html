Behind the Scenes

An Art Form, Perfected Over Generations

L’Étoile was founded in Manhattan with a singular vision: to craft a dining environment where technology and gastronomy merge into a seamless masterpiece. Guided by Chef Jean-Luc, we push structural bounds of fine-dining.

Our timeline is one of absolute dedication to high-end standards:

2018

Founding in Manhattan

L’Étoile opened its doors with a simple glass parlor and modular wood design.

Help Desk

Common Inquiries

Do you accommodate gluten-free or plant-based dietary requirements?
Yes, our fine-dining menus feature custom gluten-free, vegan, and nut-allergy notations. Please mention requirements inside our digital booking form.
What is your restaurant's dress code policy?
We maintain a premium smart-casual dress code. Collared shirts are suggested, and sportswear is discouraged in our main dining hall.
How far in advance can we book a private dining lounge?
Reservations for private event lounges open up to 90 days in advance. Call our concierge line or schedule directly through the app portal.
